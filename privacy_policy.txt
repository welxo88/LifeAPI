This is an app for private use. Users, who are not allowed separately by creator (Dmitry Poletaev),
will get automatic reponse for not eligibility.

App is intended to gather information such as food consumption via pictures shared on messenger, 
activity data from google fit and possible future sources. Data is stored on rented server without 
access for users, not allowed.
